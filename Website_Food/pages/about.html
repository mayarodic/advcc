ABOUT PAGE

This website is here to be used! The main goal of this website is to make daily cooking fun and stress free! We know the struggle of coming home from work and being indecisive on what you’d like to eat. For this reason, this website will take you through choices.

Please pick how hungry you are and if you’re craving something sweet or salty.

From that point on you will be directed to a page that will give you new recipes to try out! They change weekly which will ensure you don’t get too bored of the dishes and get to try new things.
